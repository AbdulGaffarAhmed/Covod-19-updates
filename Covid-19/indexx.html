PRECAUTIONS:-

THE BEST WAYS TO PROTECT YOURSELF

Get vaccinated for as soon as you are eligible.

Frequently wash your hands with soap and water or
use a hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol.

Wear a face mask when in public to prevent
the spread of the virus.

Cough or sneeze into a tissue or bent elbow.
Throw the tissue in the trash.

Stay at least 6 feet away from others

If you feel sick, stay home and contact
your health care provider.

Do not touch your face without washing
your hands first

Avoid crowds and poorly ventilated spaces.
Avoid indoor spaces that do not offer fresh air.

Clean and disinfect frequently touched
objects and surfaces.
